Every action takes time, measured in turns and subturns. There are 100 subturns per turn. After each full turn, Federation Agents reposition. Many actions may increase your heat level, depending on the Federation influence in your current system or planet.

Action Subturns Heat Risk Notes

Your ship's speed also affects your chance of evading Federation Agents in open space.

  • Recharging on eligible planets (for credits)

You begin the game with two emergency warp devices. Activating one instantly transports you to a random system and resets your heat level.

Each emergency warp consumes 72 energy units. If you attempt one without sufficient energy, your ship may take severe damage or be destroyed.

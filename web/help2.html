🪐 SpaceFugue Help

📜 The Story So Far

You were once captain of the INFOAWAP Excelsior, the Federation’s most advanced starship. Now you’re a fugitive—framed, hunted, and exiled.

Your mission: locate the elusive Federation supercomputer Star One, extract its secrets, and bring them to the Federation Homeworld Xaxle.

These secrets could expose a dark conspiracy: that the Federation has been compromised by the mysterious Krakkar, who seek to open a transgalactic antimatter wormhole to fulfill a deadly prophecy.

🎮 How to Play

  • Explore the galaxy by warping from system to system using hyperspace lanes.
  • Discover Star One by hacking planets and gathering intel.
  • Broadcast the truth to the galaxy at Heavy Commercial Worlds.
  • Evade 3 Federation Agents tracking your every move.
  • Manage energy, cargo, and subturns—each turn is 100 subturns worth of time.
  • Actions like travel, fuel scooping, or landing all consume subturns.

🌍 Planetary Systems

Each planet has:

  • A Federation & Tech level (influencing danger and utility)
  • Three hidden attributes: Residential, Commercial, and Industrial density (RCI)

Each RCI level unlocks certain planetary actions:

Residential Actions:
  • Light: Scout — Reveals RCI of all planets and nearby systems
  • Medium: Hack — Learn your distance and direction to Star One
  • Heavy: Spy — Reveals current location of Federation Agents
Commercial Actions:
  • Light: Shoplift — Gain credits (risk tied to Federation level)
  • Medium: Trade Mission — Discover profitable goods trade
  • Heavy: Broadcast — Broadcast Star One findings galaxy-wide
Industrial Actions:
  • Light: Repair/Recharge — Restore your ship’s energy/hull
  • Medium: Ship Modifications — Upgrade ship systems
  • Heavy: BioHack — Lower heat via genetic scrambling (at a cost)

☠️ Pirates

  • Extra fuel scooping or frequent travel risks pirate encounters.
  • Attack chance and difficulty are affected by local Federation level.
  • If shields are depleted, your hull takes damage. At zero, you lose.

🚧 Ship Modifications

Upgrade your ship to improve survival and efficiency:

  • Thrusters: Affects time to reach planets
  • Lasers: Ship damage output
  • Shields: Energy-based damage buffer
  • Hull Integrity: Final line of defense
  • Cargo: Affects trade rewards
  • Battery: Max energy storage
  • Photonic Converter: Improves fuel scoop efficiency
  • Emergency Warps: One-time escape warps

💡 Tips & Strategy

  • Use low-federation planets for illicit actions.
  • Balance ship energy with fuel availability.
  • Broadcast findings early to reduce the final mission risk.
  • Stay ahead of agent movement by lowering your heat level.
